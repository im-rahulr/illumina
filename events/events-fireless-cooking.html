Fireless Cooking

Creativity without heat – prepare delicious dishes fire-free.

Rules and Regulations

  • Maximum of two members per team.
  • Prep time: 1 hour.
  • Participants should be present 15 minutes before the allotted time.
  • Cutting and heating of vegetables or fruits by any method will not be permitted.
  • Participants must clean their respective work zones after the competitions.
  • Evaluation will be based on taste, hygiene, presentation and the name of the dish.
