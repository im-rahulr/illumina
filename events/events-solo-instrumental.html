Solo Instrumental Competition

Showcase your talent in a short solo instrumental performance.

Rules

  1. Bring your own instrument.
  2. Performance time is 2½ – 3 minutes.
  3. If you're using a backing track, please share it in advance. You can bring it in a USB or share it via WhatsApp.
  4. The judges' decision is final.
